Detailed Description

Transport PDE problem: rotation of Zalesak's slotted cylinder

See also
Zalesak, S. (1978) Fully Multidimensional Flux-Corrected Transport Algorithms for Fluids. Journal of Computational Physics 31, 335-362.
Leveque R.J. (1996) High-Resolution Conservative Algorithms for Advection in Incompressible Flow. SIAM Journal on Numerical Analyis 33, 627-665.
