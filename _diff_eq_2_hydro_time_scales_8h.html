Detailed Description

Inverse hydrodynamics time scales from DNS.

Author
J. Bakosi
Date
Fri 18 Nov 2016 08:19:06 AM MST
Copyright
2012-2015, Jozsef Bakosi, 2016, Los Alamos National Security, LLC.

Inverse hydrodynamics time scales from direct numerical simulation (DNS) of homogeneous Rayleigh-Taylor instability.

The Atwood number = 0.05 data for equilibrium time scales, invhts_eq_A005[HSL], corresponds to, and can be verified by,

# eps/k
gnuplot> plot [0.01:100] "< paste asymmetric_runs/0.05_H/M_stats/Mktaib.dat asymmetric_runs/0.05_H/M_stats/Mrhokeq.dat" u 1:((-$12)/$2) pt 7 ps 0.5 t "eps/k H, Yt=0.25", "< paste sym_runs/0.05/M_stats/Mktaib.dat sym_runs/0.05/M_stats/Mrhokeq.dat" u 1:((-$12)/$2) pt 7 ps 0.5 t "eps/k S, Yt=0.48", "< paste asymmetric_runs/0.05_L/M_stats/Mktaib.dat asymmetric_runs/0.05_L/M_stats/Mrhokeq.dat" u 1:((-$12)/$2) pt 7 ps 0.5 t "eps/k L, Yt=0.723"

The Atwood number = 0.5 data for equilibrium time scales, invhts_eq_A05[HSL], corresponds to, and can be verified by,

# eps/k
gnuplot> plot [0.01:30] "< paste asymmetric_runs/0.5_H/M_stats/Mktaib.dat asymmetric_runs/0.5_H/M_stats/Mrhokeq.dat" u 1:((-$12)/$2) pt 7 ps 0.5 t "eps/k H, Yt=0.1", "< paste sym_runs/0.5/M_stats/Mktaib.dat sym_runs/0.5/M_stats/Mrhokeq.dat" u 1:((-$12)/$2) pt 7 ps 0.5 t "eps/k S, Yt=0.25", "< paste asymmetric_runs/0.5_L/M_stats/Mktaib.dat asymmetric_runs/0.5_L/M_stats/Mrhokeq.dat" u 1:((-$12)/$2) pt 7 ps 0.5 t "eps/k L, Yt=0.49"
