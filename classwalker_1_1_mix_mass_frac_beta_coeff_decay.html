Detailed Description

Mix mass-fraction beta SDE decay coefficients policy.

User-defined parameters b' and kappa' are constants in time and ensure decay in the evolution of <y^2>.

Update coefficients using constant coefficients for b' and kappa'.

This where the mix mass-fraction beta SDE is made consistent with the no-mix and fully mixed limits by specifying the SDE coefficients, b and kappa as functions of b' and kappa'. We leave S unchanged.
