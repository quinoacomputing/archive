Based on a tet_id, decide if it's current state of locked and marked edges maps to a valid refinement case. The logic for this was derived from talking to JW and reading Chicoma.

It basically just checks if something a 1:2 and has 3 intermediates and 3 makred edges, or is a 1:4 and has 5/6 intermediates
