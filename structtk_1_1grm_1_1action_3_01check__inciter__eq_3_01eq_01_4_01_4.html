Detailed Description

template<class eq>
struct tk::grm::action< check_inciter_eq< eq > >

Do general error checking on the differential equation block.

This is error checking that all equation types must satisfy. For more specific equations, such as compressible flow, a more specialized equation checker does and can do better error checking.
